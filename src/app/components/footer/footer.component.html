About us

We are the voices of Gaza, dedicated to spreading awareness of the Israeli occupation and Genocide.

We believe that all humans are created equal, should enjoy equal human rights and be protected from bigotry and ethnic violence. This includes atheists, agnostics, Abrahamic religions followers and followers of other religions.

We do not condone terrorist actions whether from sovereign states we recognise, those we do not recognise nor from armed resistance groups. We include a link to the Hamas Charter on this page because it is typically misrepresented in the media.
